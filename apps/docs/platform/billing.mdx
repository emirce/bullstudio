## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to your organization and click **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View current usage">
    See your current plan and usage statistics:
    - Workspaces used
    - Connections used
    - Alerts enabled
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Click **Upgrade** on the Pro or Enterprise plan card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    You'll be redirected to our secure payment portal (powered by Polar).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access new features">
    Once payment is complete, new limits and features are available immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Current Plan

1. Go to **Billing** in your organization
2. Your current plan is shown with a badge
3. Usage statistics show how much of your limits you're using

### Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. You'll be redirected to the billing portal
4. Update your payment method

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Click **Cancel Subscription** in the portal
4. Your access will continue until the end of the billing period

<Warning>
  When your subscription ends, your organization will be downgraded to Free. If you exceed Free plan limits, you'll need to delete workspaces/connections or resubscribe.
</Warning>

### View Invoices

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. View and download past invoices in the portal

## Limits and Enforcement

### Approaching Limits

You can use up to your plan's limits:

- **Free**: 1 workspace, 1 connection
- **Pro**: 5 workspaces, 5 connections per workspace
- **Enterprise**: 10 workspaces, 10 connections per workspace

### At Limit

When you reach your limit:

- You cannot create additional workspaces or connections
- Existing resources continue to work
- Upgrade to create more

### Exceeding Limits (Downgrade)

If you downgrade and exceed the new plan's limits:

- Existing resources remain accessible
- You cannot create new resources until under limit
- Delete resources or upgrade to unlock creation
